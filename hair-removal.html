Hair Removal

At Usha Beauty, we provide various hair removal treatments designed to suit your needs and preferences. From precise threading to effective waxing, our experienced professionals use high-quality products to ensure a smooth, flawless finish. Choose the best option for your skin type and enjoy lasting results that leave your skin feeling soft and rejuvenated.

Threading


Threading is a precise method of hair removal that uses a thin thread to remove hair from the root. It is perfect for shaping eyebrows, upper lips, and other sensitive areas.

Price: RM 30 - RM 80

Waxing


Waxing is an effective method for removing hair from larger areas, such as the legs, arms, and bikini line. It leaves your skin smooth and hair-free for weeks at a time.

Price: RM 50 - RM 150
